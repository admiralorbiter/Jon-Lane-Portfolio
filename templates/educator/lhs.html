Lawrence High School

I taught math and computer science which broke down into 4 different preps: Advance Algebra 2, Algebra 2, Intro to CS, Advance CS. This was my first year teaching, so I had a tenuous grasp on the students at the best of times.

My white board became a place for student creativity. All it took the spark of one student to start drawing on the board and others took off. Throughout the year I would have to clear them, but that didn't stop the artists from continuing their work. I love the idea of impermanence and white board was a great reminder.

I had a great batch of students, especially for year 1, even though they did toe the line. I even had a group dress as me for Halloween! At the end of the year my 5th hour even got me a cake with my 'catch phrase' they had been hearing all semester.

I did not have to answer any question.
