About Me

PREP-KC - Innovation Technology Manager

Masters of CS, ASU, 2023

As an enthusiastic educator, adept programmer, and fierce competitor, I embrace these roles with a dynamic spirit—often finding them intertwined in my daily life. At present, I work with a nonprofit, where I help orchestrate a computer science pathway for several school districts across the bistate area. It's truly inspiring to guide students as they discover the power and potential of coding.

Programming is more than a career to me—it's a calling. I'm always on the lookout to learn and apply the latest technologies, contributing to open source projects and finding innovative ways to solve problems. This ongoing journey of learning and sharing knowledge is what drives me every day.

Outside of my professional endeavors, I immerse myself in the world of competitive gaming, from strategic card games like poker to esports like StarCraft 2. This not only satisfies my competitive spirit but also hones skills that are vital in both personal and professional contexts—like strategic planning and quick decision-making.

In everything I do, I aim to inspire, innovate, and push boundaries, while keeping the fun and passion alive in all my pursuits.
